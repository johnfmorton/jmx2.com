A New Chapter

After more than 15 years of creating digital experiences for some of the world's most recognized brands, JMX2 has closed its doors.

From our start in 2008 to our final project, we had the privilege of working with incredible teams at Google, Salesforce, Coca-Cola, HP, Macy's, Verizon, and many others. We crafted animations, built applications, designed interfaces, and pushed the boundaries of what was possible at the intersection of code and creativity.

To every client, collaborator, and colleague who trusted us with their vision—thank you.

Still Building

While JMX2 has reached its conclusion, we haven't stopped creating. John and Jon continue to work as independent developers, and still team up when the right projects come along.
